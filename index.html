Classic Waffles

Ingredients

  • 2 cups of flour
  • 2 tbsp of sugar
  • 2 tsp of baking powder
  • 1/2 tsp of salt
  • 2 tbspn of corn starch
  • 3 eggs
  • 4 tbsp of melted butter
  • 1 and 3/4 cups of milk
  • 1 tbsp of vanilla essence

Instructions

  1. Sieve the flour, sugar, starch, baking powder and the salt in a bowl.
  2. In a different bowl mix the eggs, milk, butter and vanilla.
  3. Add the flour mixture and blend the ingredients.
  4. Heat the waffle machine. Add a ladle of the mixture and spread on the machine, close it and wait until it browns.
  5. Remove the waffle from the machine and serve with honey, fruits, jam or maple syrup.

Additional information

You can serve this waffle at breakfast with jam.
